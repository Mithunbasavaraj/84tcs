Kashmir 5N/6D

Duration

10 Days, 11 Nights

Location

Kashmir 4N/5D

Arrival: Arrive at Srinagar airport and meet our representative. Transfer to your hotel or houseboat for check-in.
Sightseeing: Visit the famous Mughal Gardens: Nishat Bagh (Garden of Pleasure), Shallmar Bagh (Abode of Love), and Chashme Shahl (Royal Spring). Explore the Shankaracharya Temple on the Zabarwan Hills for a panoramic view of Srinagar.
Stroll along the shores of the Dal Lake and enjoy a Shikara ride.
Evening: Retum to the houseboat or hotel for dinner and overnight stay.

Moming: After breakfast, proceed to Gulmarg, known as the "Meadow of Flowers.”
Activities: Take the Gondola Ride (cable car) to enjoy breathtaking views of the snow-clad mountains. Enjoy optional activities like skiing, snowboarding, or trekking (seasonal).
Evening: Retum to Srinagar for dinner and ovemight stay.

Moming: Post breakfast, drive to Pahalgam, the "Valley of Shepherds.”
En Route: Visit the Saffron Fields and the historic Awantipora Ruins.
Activities: Explore the picturesque Aru Valley, Betaab Valley, and Lidder River (optional pony rides), Enjoy nature walks or relax by the riverside.
Evening: Check in to your hotel in Pahalgam for dinner and overnight stay.

Morning: After breakfast, proceed for a full-day excursion to Sonmarg, the "Meadow of Gold.”
Activities: Visit Thajiwas Glacier (accessible by pony rides or trekking).
Take in the serene views of the Sindh River.
Evening: Retum to Srinagar for dinner and overnight stay.

Moming: After breakfast, check out from the hotel.
Transfer: Drive to Srinagar airport for your onward journey.
